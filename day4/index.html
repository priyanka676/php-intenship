Ahmedabad

The largest city in Gujarat state of India, Ahmedabad is a major industrial city that is popularly referred as the 'Manchester of the East'. The History of Ahmedabad Gujarat is a checkered and eventful one. The city has been under various rulers since its foundation and thus boasts of a rich history. Indianholiday offers detailed online information on the History of Ahmedabad Gujarat. Located on the banks of the River Sabarmati in western India, Ahmedabad was the former capital of Gujarat and has been home to some of the most important leaders of India's independence movement like Mahatma Gandhi and Sardar Patel. Ahmedabad in Gujarat is also famous as the cultural and economic hub of Gujarat.
